Steps to bake a cake

  1. Gather all ingredients (flour, sugar, eggs, butter/oil, baking powder, milk, vanilla).
  2. Prepare cake pan (grease, flour, or line with parchment paper).
  3. Cream sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
  4. Cream sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
  5. Sift flour, baking powder, and salt (if used).
  6. Alternate adding dry ingredients and milk to wet mixture, mix gently.
  7. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth top.
  8. Bake for 25–40 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pan for 5–10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
  10. Frost and decorate once completely cool.

Porgraming Languages

C:
A powerful, low-level programming language mainly used for system programming and operating system development.
C++:
An extension of C that includes object-oriented features for building complex software with high performance.
Python:
A versatile, high-level language known for its readability and wide use in web development, data science, and automation.
JAVA:
A platform-independent, object-oriented programming language commonly used for building enterprise-scale applications.
Javascript:
A scripting language primarily used to create interactive and dynamic content on websites.
Lua:
A lightweight, embeddable scripting language often used for game development and embedded systems.
HTML:
A markup language used to structure and present content on the web.
CSS:
A stylesheet language used to describe the appearance and formatting of a document written in HTML.
SQL:
A specialized language for managing and querying relational databases.
